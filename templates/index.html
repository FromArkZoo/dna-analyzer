Pharma

Pharmacogenomics

Your genes affect how your body processes medications. Some people break down drugs faster or slower than average.

Understanding Metabolizer Types

Poor Metabolizer — Drug builds up; stronger side effects possible.

Intermediate — Somewhat slower processing; may need lower dose.

Normal — Standard dosing is likely appropriate.

Rapid / Ultra-rapid — Drug processed quickly; may be less effective.
